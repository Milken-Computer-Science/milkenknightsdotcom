Achievements

Each year, teams compete by building robots that complete a certain task in a new competition. However, awards are not only given to the team who wins the competition, but additionally, awards are given for other achievements such as being a finalist or for having the most team spirit. Here is a list of all the awards our team has won.

    • 2017 Dean's List Finalist Award (Jared Hasen-Klein) Idaho Regional
    • 2016 Finalists Orange County Regional
    • 2016 Gracious Professionalism® Award sponsored by Johnson & Johnson Orange County Regional
    • 2014 Champions Fall Classic
    • 2013 Finalists MadTown ThrowDown
    • 2013 Champions Los Angeles Regional
    • 2012 Finalist Los Angeles Regional
    • 2009 Autodesk Visualization Award LA Regional
    • 2010 Dean's List Finalist Award (Nathan Schloss) LA Regional
    • 2008 Chrysler Team Spirit Award LA Regional
